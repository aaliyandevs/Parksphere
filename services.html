National Parks

Karachi Safari Park

An exciting urban safari experience where you can see lions, elephants, and zebras. The park offers a lake, chairlift rides, animal enclosures, and kid-friendly areas with fun outdoor activities.

Lal Suhanra National Park

One of Pakistan's largest parks, featuring desert, forest, and wetland zones. Home to blackbucks, deer, and rare birds. Ideal for family picnics, wildlife viewing, and peaceful nature exploration.

Ayub National Park

A historic park with a mini-zoo, open green spaces, and wildlife like deer, peacocks, and monkeys. Families enjoy gardens, walking tracks, and a variety of children's entertainment zones.
